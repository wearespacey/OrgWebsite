ABOUT US

SpaceY was a public Belgian organization that focuses on delivering opensource projects based on new technologies.

These projects were from different areas, answer or redefine different needs and spontaneously break implicit standards in order to show the developer community

that we can still improve current applications but also create new things !

SpaceY was active during 2 long years between 2019 and 2021. Now the members are most of the time still working on open source but doesn't have time anymore to work on SpaceY.
